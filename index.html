How to reduce consumption

Electricity

  • Turn off unused equipment

Water

  • Fix leaks quickly
  • Install low‑flow taps
  • Reuse greywater when possible

Office supplies

  • Print only when necessary
  • Use recycled paper
  • Reuse folders and materials

Cleaning products

  • Buy concentrated products
  • Use refillable containers
  • Optimize cleaning schedules

30% Reduction Plan (3-Year Strategy)

Detailed Reduction Plan

This section explains in depth how the reduction measures work, how savings are calculated, and how the school can progressively reach a 70% reduction in consumption.

How the reduction model works

  • Each category (electricity, water, office, cleaning) has 5 measures.
  • Each measure has a different impact (weight) on consumption.
  • The user selects the measures they want to implement.
  • The calculator applies the combined reduction (up to a maximum of 70%).

Why 70% is achievable

The combination of structural improvements (solar panels, low‑flow taps, digitalisation) and behavioural changes (awareness, correct usage) allows the school to reach a realistic 70% reduction over several years.

Implementation timeline

  • Year 1: Quick wins (LED, sensors, leak repairs, digitalisation)
  • Year 2: Optimisation and monitoring
  • Year 3: Structural upgrades and consolidation
