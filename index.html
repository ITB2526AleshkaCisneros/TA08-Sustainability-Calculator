How to reduce consumption

Electricity

  • Turn off unused equipment
  • Optimize AC/heating
  • Use energy‑efficient devices

Water

  • Fix leaks quickly
  • Install low‑flow taps
  • Reuse greywater when possible

Office supplies

  • Print only when necessary
  • Use recycled paper
  • Reuse folders and materials

Cleaning products

  • Buy concentrated products
  • Use refillable containers
  • Optimize cleaning schedules

30% Reduction Plan (3-Year Strategy)

🎯 Main Objective

Reduce electricity, water, office supplies and cleaning products consumption by 30% within 3 years.

🛠️ Concrete Actions

  • Replace lighting with LED and optimise usage schedules.
  • Install water-saving devices and repair leaks quickly.
  • Digitise documents and reduce unnecessary printing.
  • Use concentrated and refillable cleaning products.
  • Reuse materials and prioritise sustainable purchasing.

📏 Indicators

  • kWh consumed per year
  • Litres of water per year
  • Units of office supplies per year
  • Annual economic cost
  • % reduction compared to baseline year
